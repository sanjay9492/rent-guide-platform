The "11-Month" Rule

Most agreements are for 11 months to bypass mandatory registration. If you plan to stay longer, ask for a registered lease for safety.

üé®

Painting Charges

Landlords in Bengaluru often deduct 1 month's rent for "painting" upon exit. innovative! Negotiate this clause before signing.

üëÆ

Police Verification

It's mandatory in many cities. PGs usually handle this, but for flats, ensure the owner submits your details to the local station.

No Brokers!

Never pay a "visiting fee" to see a house. Genuine brokers charge only after deal closure. On RentGuide, you pay zero brokerage.

Water & Maintenance

Check if water charges are fixed or metered. In summer, tanker costs can spike maintenance bills significantly.
